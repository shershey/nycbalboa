# Learn

## Beginners

If you are not a Balboa beginner, the YSBD intermediate and advanced classes are great. Check them out! If you are new to Balboa there are several options to you to start learning in NYC. The following are a few ideas:

1. During most of the year, You Should Be Dancing offers drop in beginner balboa classes. They sometimes put them on hold during the summer. Group classes are great for learning to dance but also a great way to meet other people who are excited to learn.
2. Schedule a private lesson with one of the teachers below. Private lessons are one of the best ways to learn to dance. While they're pricier than classes, the teachers can go at exactly your pace. All the instructors below are great.
3. If you're an experienced social dancer or know a little Balboa, the Thursday practice could be an interesting and less structured way to learn. From 7:30-8:30, other dancers might be available to help show you some of the basics. From 8:30-9:30 the hosts are available to help anyone with questions and can show you some stuff for a few minutes inbetween helping others.
