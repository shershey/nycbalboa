# Begin

New to Balboa in NYC? Here are a few ways to get started:

1. *You Should Be Dancing Crash Courses*: There is currently one scheduled for [July 11 and 12](https://www.facebook.com/share/14jn2ckvSZS/)
2. *You Should Be Dancing Tuesday Beginner Classes*: During most of the year, You Should Be Dancing offers drop-in beginner Balboa classes. They sometimes put them on hold during the summer. Group classes are great for learning to dance and a great way to meet other people who are excited to learn.
2. *Private Lessons*: Schedule a private lesson with one of the teachers on the [Learn](/offerings) page. Private lessons are one of the best ways to learn to dance. While they're pricier than classes, the teachers can go at exactly your pace.
3. *Practice Session*: If you're an experienced social dancer or know a little Balboa, the [Thursday Practice](https://thursdaypractice.nycbalboa.com) could be an interesting and less structured way to learn. From 7:30–8:30, other dancers might be available to help show you some of the basics. From 8:30–9:30 the hosts are available to help anyone with questions.

If you are not a Balboa beginner, the YSBD intermediate and advanced classes are great — check them out! See the [Learn](/offerings) page for more options.
